Welcome to the Quantum Circuit Zoo
A guide to actual implementations of key quantum algorithms
Welcome to the Quantum Circuits Zoo! For now, these are mostly personal notes that I aim to mold into a more useful online reference. Mainly, I’m annoyed that people don’t add circuit examples when explaining algorithms or writing about them in papers. This aims to fix this. The pages with Emojis like this () are the work in progress pages, while () are pages that I’m reasonably happy with. Most pages are empty, and many page don’t even exist yet. If you are interested in contributing, or have questions, please contact Thomas Steckmann. I leave it to you to find an appropriate means, because I don’t want this to show up in an email crawler. Try Twitter, LinkedIn, or my personal website.
Right now, this website is basically a Pre-Alpha. Honestly, I’m surprised you found it in the first place. I’m mostly trying to see how it shows up in search results, and also play around with the web-hosting elements of the website. I’m using Notion to generate the pages and write the content, and Git-Hub pages + some wonderful repos to compile the notion pages into a static site hosted at a custom address. I’ll probably leave it up though in case it gets any traffic.
