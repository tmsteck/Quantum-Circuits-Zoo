Theory of Trotter Error with Commutator Scaling
Abstract The Lie-Trotter formula, together with its higher-order generalizations, provides a direct approach to decomposing the exponential of a sum of operators. Despite significant effort, the error scaling of such product formulas remains poorly understood. We develop a theory of Trotter error that overcomes the limitations of prior approaches based on truncating the Baker-Campbell-Hausdorff expansion.
https://journals.aps.org/prx/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevX.11.011020
